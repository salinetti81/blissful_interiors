Giving

"We make a living by what we get.
We make a life by what we give"
Winston S. Churchill

As a lover of all animals, Blissful Interiors donates a portion of its profits to the Melrose Humane society to contribute to their mission of rescuing homeless cats.

Melrose Humane Society (MHS) is a group of volunteers who take in sick, abandoned, injured and unwanted animals. We currently care for cats that we take into our own homes, get them the medical treatment they need and care for them until they are ready for adoption.
